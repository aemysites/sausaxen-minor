Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any special offers available when booking with Minor Hotels?

We regularly feature special offers on this page, which are updated from time to time – so be sure to check back often. The easiest way to receive an offer when booking with Minor Hotels is to join Minor DISCOVERY, our loyalty programme. Members enjoy up to 10% savings on stays, along with exclusive benefits such as special promotions, early or late check-out, room upgrades and more. As a Minor DISCOVERY member, you'll also get early access to limited sales and Member Days offers. Plus, take advantage of promotions involving DISCOVERY Dollars (D$), including double D$ rewards at newly opened hotels and limited-time bonus D$ deals.

Discover more benefits of Minor DISCOVERY.

What types of hotel offers are available at Minor Hotels?
Apart from our exclusive offer for Minor DISCOVERY members, there are other promotions you can enjoy – including breakfast-inclusive deals, earning DISCOVERY Dollars (D$) with your stay, and double D$ rewards when booking our newest hotels and resorts. You can also access discounted rates by booking in advance through our Advance Purchase promotions.
How long are Minor Hotels' current offers valid?
It depends on the type of promotional offer. DISCOVERY Dollars (D$) earned through stays are generally valid for 24 months from the date they are awarded, although this may vary depending on your membership level. Some seasonal promotions are only available for a limited time. Full details are provided with each offer, so be sure to read the terms carefully to make the most of our hotel deals.
What benefits do Minor DISCOVERY members enjoy when booking directly through the Minor Hotels website and app?

As a Minor DISCOVERY member, booking directly on the Minor Hotels website and app unlocks exclusive Web Privileges. These privileges include:

  • Up to 10% savings on stays
  • 5 bonus D$ earned with each web booking
  • 20 bonus D$ earned after each 4th stay booked on website
  • Free premium WiFi
  • Access to exclusive member-only offers

These benefits are available globally across all participating Minor Hotels when you book online as a Minor DISCOVERY member.

Are offers available across all brands of Minor Hotels?
Yes, many promotions are available across all Minor Hotel brands. However, some offers may be limited to specific brands, regions or hotels. We recommend checking this page for the latest list of offers before you book your preferred destination. This ensures you get the best rate for your stay, every time.
How can I become a member and enjoy Minor DISCOVERY benefits?
It's simple. Membership is free and takes only a minute to complete. Just sign up for Minor DISCOVERY to get started. Once you've joined, you'll unlock exclusive perks, member benefits and more.
Are luxury hotel deals available at all Minor Hotels' destinations?
At Minor Hotels, we offer a wide range of luxury brands, with some promotions valid only at specific destinations or hotels. Promotion dates may also vary by property due to high demand. Keep an eye on the latest offers from Minor Hotels to make the most of these limited-time opportunities.
Can I combine multiple offers in one booking?
Yes, you can combine some of our hotel deals, such as up to 10% off for Minor DISCOVERY members and seasonal offers. As always, be sure to read the terms and conditions for full details.
Are there any deals for business travellers at Minor Hotels?
Yes, we offer special deals for professionals through our corporate discount programme. These apply to a wide range of professionals, from companies and travel agencies to individual business travellers booking meeting rooms for events or conferences. You can find details at minorpro.com.
